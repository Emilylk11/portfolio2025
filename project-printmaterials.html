Print & Product Design

Bringing brands to life through tangible design—one flyer, banner, and booth at a time.

Overview

This case study highlights my work in creating high-impact print materials and product design assets for events, workshops, and ongoing brand needs. From retractable banners to one-pagers and recruitment flyers, I translated digital branding into tactile designs that resonated with both internal teams and external audiences.

Project Scope

Led the design and layout for a variety of print materials including career fair banners, tabletop signage, branded templates for one-pagers, as well as recruitment and capabilities flyers. My responsibilities included copywriting, layout design, vendor coordination, and final asset export in both print-ready and digital formats.

My Process

Step 1: Aligned on messaging and layout requirements with stakeholders
Step 2: Designed and iterated drafts in Illustrator and InDesign
Step 3: Proofed print dimensions and print-shop compatibility
Step 4: Delivered press-ready PDFs and optimized versions for digital sharing
